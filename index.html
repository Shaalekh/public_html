MASAI FORCE

Wakanda Forever!

Introduction

The armed branch of NMSC is Masai Force. 'Masai Force' derive its name from the Masai tribe of East Africa. The language spoken here is Maa language. Since Wakanda has established friendly relations with outer world, Masai Force has accepted the proposal of training of foreign cadets here.

History

In the 5th century, a tribe of African warriors and conquerors came to Wakanda, following a path of victory. Later that tribe got to known as Masai Tribe. By the time passed, Wakanda evolved so as Masai tribe. Since her birth Wakanda has been fighting against the aquisitiveness of capturing her of outer world.

Training

Cadets are called 'Ikadethi' and lady cadets are called 'Tazuni' in Maa language. Ikadethis are trained at Sigma Academy, located at Wakanda. From traditional tribal fighting skills to modern combat, the training given is world class. Each Ikadethi is sized up in every condition and moulded into a war machine of steel.
